3. **Analysis by Order Status:**

4. **Top Performing Brands:**
   - Top 3 brands by revenue generated
   - For each brand: name, total revenue, quantity of products sold

5. **Insights and Recommendations:**
   - Main observations about financial performance
   - Improvement suggestions based on the data
